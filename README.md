## About Me

Hi, I’m **Dennis Kontic**, a cybersecurity graduate and Blue Team practitioner focused on SOC analysis, digital forensics, incident response, threat hunting, malware analysis, threat intelligence, and detection engineering.

I graduated from **Lindenwood University** with a **Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity** and a **Minor in Business Administration**, earning a **3.94 GPA**. My background includes professional experience as a **Level 1 IT Technician at CarShield**, **Information Security Intern at Ascend Learning**, **Information Systems Skilled Support Intern with St. Charles County Government**, and **Cybersecurity Content Creator through DonkeySec**.

Through **DonkeySec**, I create blue team focused cybersecurity content that helps learners understand practical security investigations. My work includes lab walkthroughs, technical writeups, defensive security tutorials, and project documentation across platforms such as Hack The Box, TryHackMe, CyberDefenders, Blue Team Labs Online, Microsoft Sentinel, Splunk, ANY.RUN, Wireshark, Volatility, Autopsy, and other security tools.
